Overview

Purpose

White Water Rafting, a company specialising in rafting and tourism, is ready to provide you with an unforgettable experience.

Audience

Who are the target customers? People with a thirst for adventure, looking for more contact with nature. It can be practised by almost anyone, including children from the age of 8 with a companion, subject to the following restrictions: not being pregnant, not having a heart condition, being physically fit, not having drunk alcohol or taken drugs.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Everything you need to know and how to prepare for this adventure, and some questions such as: what is rafting, how did it come about, who can practise it, package prices, etc.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Mobile device
